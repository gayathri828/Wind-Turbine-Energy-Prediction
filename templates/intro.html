Wind Turbine Energy Prediction Based on Weather Conditions

Wind energy is one of the most important renewable energy sources used today. The power generated by wind turbines depends strongly on weather conditions, especially wind speed and atmospheric behavior.

This project uses machine learning techniques to predict the energy output of wind turbines based on wind speed and theoretical power values. Such predictions help energy providers improve forecasting accuracy, optimize grid integration, and plan turbine maintenance efficiently.

Key Applications:
• Energy production forecasting
• Smart grid load balancing
• Maintenance scheduling for wind turbines
